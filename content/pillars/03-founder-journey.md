# Pillar 3: Founder Journey

Behind-the-scenes, personal brand, staying small deliberately.

## Post Ideas

### 1. I left a big BC partner to start a 3-person firm. Here's what I learned.
- **Hook:** "I left a big BC partner to start a 3-person firm. Everyone said I was crazy. Here's what happened."
- **Format:** Text post (story-driven)
- **Key point:** Why going small was intentional — focus on quality, direct client relationships, no layers of management

- **Status:** Not drafted

### 2. Why we'll never hire juniors
- **Hook:** "We'll never hire juniors. Not because they're bad. Because our model doesn't work with them."
- **Format:** Text post (contrarian)
- **Key point:** Senior-only means every person on the project can make decisions, talk to stakeholders, and ship independently. Training juniors requires a different business model.
- **Angle:** Contrarian but respectful — not anti-junior, just pro-specialization
- **Status:** Not drafted

## Notes

- These posts build trust through vulnerability and honesty
- Avoid humble-bragging — tell real stories with real challenges
- Personal photos or behind-the-scenes shots increase engagement
