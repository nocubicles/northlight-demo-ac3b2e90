# Pillar 5: Client Outcomes

Case studies, ERP decision-making, questions to ask your BC partner.

## Post Ideas

### 1. 5 questions to ask before choosing a Business Central partner
- **Hook:** "Choosing a BC partner? Ask these 5 questions before signing anything."
- **Format:** Text post
- **Key point:**
  1. Who exactly will work on my project? (seniors or juniors?)
  2. What's your approach to customization vs. configuration?
  3. How do you handle scope changes?
  4. What does post-go-live support look like?
  5. Can I talk to a recent client?
- **Angle:** Helpful, positions Integrated.ee without being salesy
- **Status:** Not drafted

## Notes

- These posts attract potential clients at the decision-making stage
- Be genuinely helpful — recommend alternatives when BC isn't the right fit
- Avoid case study posts that feel like ads — focus on the buyer's perspective
- "Questions to ask" format works well for engagement
