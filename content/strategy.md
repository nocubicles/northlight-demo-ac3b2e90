# LinkedIn Content Strategy

## Goal

Build Villem's personal brand as a senior BC consultant who runs an AI-native, deliberately small consultancy in Estonia. Drive awareness for Integrated.ee and attract clients who value expertise over headcount.

## Content Pillars

### 1. BC Technical Tips

Repurpose blog posts into bite-sized tips. Show deep technical knowledge.

- **Source:** `homepage/posts/` (30+ existing articles)
- **Format:** Text + code screenshot, or carousel for complex topics
- **Angle:** "Here's something most BC devs miss" — practical, immediately useful
- **Frequency:** ~1x/week

### 2. AI-Native Consulting

How AI changes the BC consulting workflow. This is Integrated.ee's key differentiator.

- **Topics:** AI-assisted AL development, AI in project delivery, honest takes on what AI can/can't do
- **Format:** Text posts, polls
- **Angle:** Practical, no hype — "AI writes our code. Humans review every line."
- **Frequency:** ~2x/month

### 3. Founder Journey

Personal stories, behind-the-scenes, staying small deliberately.

- **Topics:** Why senior-only, leaving a big partner, daily life of a 3-person BC firm
- **Format:** Text posts (story-driven)
- **Angle:** Honest, sometimes contrarian — "Why we'll never hire juniors"
- **Frequency:** ~2x/month

### 4. Estonian ERP Market

Local compliance, market data, BC adoption in Estonia.

- **Topics:** Estonian e-invoicing, localization needs, market size, competition
- **Format:** Text posts, carousels with data
- **Angle:** Local expertise, data-driven
- **Frequency:** ~2x/month

### 5. Client Outcomes

Before/after scenarios, questions to ask your BC partner, decision frameworks.

- **Topics:** ERP selection criteria, implementation success stories, honest cost discussions
- **Format:** Text posts, blog links
- **Angle:** Helpful, consultative — help buyers make better decisions
- **Frequency:** ~2x/month

## Cadence

**3x/week: Monday, Wednesday, Friday**

| Day       | Pillar Focus                         |
|-----------|--------------------------------------|
| Monday    | Technical or AI-native (Pillar 1/2)  |
| Wednesday | Founder journey or market (Pillar 3/4) |
| Friday    | Client outcomes or engagement (Pillar 5 / poll) |

## Formats

| Format                    | Frequency   | Notes                                    |
|---------------------------|-------------|------------------------------------------|
| Text post (story-driven)  | Most common | 1200-1500 chars, hook in first 2 lines   |
| Text + code screenshot    | 2-3x/month  | For technical tips                       |
| Carousel (PDF, 5-8 slides)| 1x/month    | For blog repurposing, data-heavy topics  |
| Poll                      | 1x/month    | For engagement, market research          |
| Article with blog link    | 1-2x/month  | Drive traffic to integrated.ee           |

## Voice Guidelines

- **First person** — Always Villem's voice, never corporate "we" (except when referring to the team)
- **Direct, no fluff** — Get to the point. No "In today's fast-paced world..."
- **Confident but honest** — Share expertise freely, admit limits openly
- **Occasionally contrarian** — "Most BC partners staff juniors. We don't." Challenge assumptions.
- **Practical over theoretical** — Every post should have a takeaway
- **No hashtag spam** — 3-5 relevant hashtags max, at the end

## Post Structure

1. **Hook** (first 2 lines) — Must make people click "see more"
2. **Story/insight** — The main content, keep it scannable
3. **Takeaway** — What the reader should do or think differently
4. **CTA** (optional) — Question, link, or invitation to comment
