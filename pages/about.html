About us

Building technology with intention

integrated.ee is a boutique technology consulting firm based in Tallinn, Estonia. We help organizations navigate complex technology decisions with clarity, precision, and a commitment to lasting results.

Founder

Villem Heinsalu

A Microsoft MVP in Business Applications with over 15 years of experience in ERP and technology consulting. Villem brings deep expertise in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central, systems integration, and solution architecture.

His career spans enterprise implementations across Europe and North America, working with organizations that need more than off-the-shelf solutions — they need strategic technology partnerships.

As the founder of integrated.ee, Villem leads every engagement with a focus on understanding the full picture: business context, technical constraints, and the people who will use the systems every day.

Our principles

What guides our work

Precision over speed

Every decision exists within a larger context. We consider the whole system — technology, processes, and people.

No black boxes. We work alongside your team, sharing knowledge and building capability as we go.

The best solutions are the simplest ones that work. We resist unnecessary complexity at every turn.
